About security and privacy

Note that your email address and your password are encrypted using the sha1-algorithm and stored in a graph in GVS-Service. Currently, these encrypted strings are publicly accessible. This means that your password is not safe against dictionary attacks and that a person having your email address can find out that you registered with this service. The email address itself is only used for the sending of a single verification email and is not stored.

Also note that all the triples you add to this GVS-Service, either by entering them directly, through an application, or by initiating their aggregation on the web, will be publicly accessible. While they can be removed-at-time-T from the graph they cannot be completely removed as they will always be accessible through old versions of the graph.
